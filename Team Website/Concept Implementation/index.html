Overview

The final goal of this assignment was to create a Sci-Fi themed experience and accompanying “gags” to be implemented by the 5 Wits Company, a themed, indoor, interactive adventure park company. Adventures already employed by 5 Wits include an “Espionage” adventure where users act as spies on an important mission and an adventure that takes place “20,000 Leagues Under the Sea” where users solve puzzles in a Jules Verne-themed series of “submarine” rooms, among several other adventures.

Project Review:

The purpose of the Time Helm is for users the “steer their way to a specific destination in time.” The device acts as a puzzle with four rotating rings that must be arranged by the user into a specific configuration.

Upon solving the puzzle and arranging the four rotating rings in their correct positions, a door to another time will be unlocked and the users will be allowed to advance to the next room or puzzle.
