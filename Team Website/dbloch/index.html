Dan Bloch

This is a sketch model of a puzzle concept that could be implemented to activate our time machine.

The tiles slide around the console so that any arrangment of the symbols could be made.

Based upon given information, users would have to place either one symbol tile into the activation space, or align the tiles into a particular arrangement o activate the time machine.

This puzzle console could be implemented in many places and on almost any surface. The following group of users could easily be given different information so that a different tile or arrangement of tiles would activate that time machine.
