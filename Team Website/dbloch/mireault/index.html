Al Mireault

Delorean time machine (polystyrene foam)

Concept Background:

Delorean time machine concept for 5wits based on back to the future. Time selection device would be placed in the interior of the car, and the selctions made by users would effect the exterior of the vehicle.

Implementation:

Sketch model hand carved from green polystyreme foam and based on 1982 D-12 model.
