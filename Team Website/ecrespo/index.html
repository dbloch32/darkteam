Circular Time Travel Room

Background

The idea is for the 5Wits group to come into a circular room, a Time Travel Room. The room design is based out of the Catching Fire Clock Arena, which is divided into 12 equal segments. Similar to the movie, each segment of the room will be activated at a different time. This means that every user will have to pay attention to when their section will be activated (possibly indicated by ilumitating the radii that border their section).

Each section will have a series of circular dials on the wall. These dials will be rotated in order to select a symboled time. The symbols to be selected will be displayed somewhere in the room on a "Time Display Device" that we will design in the next phase. We plan on placing this display on the middle of the room, so everyone can see it from their respective locations.

The room is envisioned to be 25 feet in diameter and 10 feet tall. To come in, a sliding door will open, such that when it slides closed it keeps the symetry of the 12 sections. To exit, another sliding door will open, either to the future or to the past.
