Concept Exploration Models

Design, build and test simple "works-like" and/or "looks-like" models of possible user experience concepts.

The Dark Team decided to follow up with four different aspects to test for this phase of the concept exploration. Eric's Time Room design was represented with a 1/30 scale model of a circular room divided radially into 12 sections, represting 12 hours on a typical clock, which will play a part in his puzzle. Dan's Sliding Tile Puzzle was built using foam core to show a simple design of how the puzzle would function. Alice's and Ayantu's Time Selection Podium models were built using foam core and laser-cut acrylic. Al's 1981 DeLorean model was sculpted and shaped from a dense green foam.
